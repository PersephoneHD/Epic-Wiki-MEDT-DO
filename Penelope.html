Penelope is the queen of Ithaca and wife of Odysseus as well as Telemechus mother.

In the Ithaca Saga

Story

The Ithaca Saga starts with her tricks to stall the suitors and her challenge, a last attempt to buy Odysseus more time to return Home. The following songs show the events that unfold as a result of her husbands return and their long awaited reunion

The Songs:

Penelope sings or is mentioned in these Songs

  1. The Challenge
  2. Hold them Down
  3. Odysseus
  4. I Can´t Help but Wonder
  5. Would You fall in Love with Me Again

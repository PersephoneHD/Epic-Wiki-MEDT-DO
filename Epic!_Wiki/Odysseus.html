Odysseus is the King of Ithaca and a captain in the greek army. He is the protagonist in the musical Epic! and the main hero of it and Homer´s Odyssee. His wife is Penelope and his son´s name is Telemachus.

In the Ithaca Saga

Story

After 20 years away from home, and many tragic events, Odysseus finally makes it back home to Ithaca. To his dismay he finds it besieged with suitors that urge Penelope to take a new husband as they assume him to be dead. In this Saga Odysseus deals with the suuitors to finally reunite with his family afterwards.
